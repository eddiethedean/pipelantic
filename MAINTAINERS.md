## Responsibilities

- Merge decisions for public API, plugin protocols, and release tags
- Security report triage per [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md)
- Code of Conduct enforcement per [docs/11_DEVELOPMENT/CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md](docs/11_DEVELOPMENT/CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md)
- Roadmap sequencing (non-binding dates)

## Becoming a maintainer

Maintainers are appointed by the lead maintainer based on sustained,
high-quality contributions and demonstrated judgment on compatibility and
security. Inactive maintainers may be removed after documented notice.

See [Governance](docs/11_DEVELOPMENT/GOVERNANCE.md).
